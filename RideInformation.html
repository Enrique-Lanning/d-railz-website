Ride Information

Levitator:
This joyful slow spinning ride makes your child believe they are flying high. It features horses and other animals that move up and down to imitate the feeling of flying.

This ride is targetted towards the 6-10 age range and parents must ensure their child can hold on to the bars.
DragCoaster:
This rollercoaster is guaranteed to mimic the sensation of teenagers riding a dragon. This ride features low arching tracks at low speeds.

The dedicated age range is 11-15 and occupants must be of appropiate height.
Looping Flights:
This chaotic rollercoaster will certainly give a feeling of jetlag. Features consist of high drops and frequent loops.

This ride is targetted towards the 16-18 age range and exclusive to three day ticket holders.
Carriages:
This turning wheel ensures a gorgeous view of the theme park and the Cardiff area. It features carriages that rotate to the highest point of 85 metres.

This ride is targetted towards families and parents must ensure children do not have a fear of heights.
