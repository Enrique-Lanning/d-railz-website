About Us

Before the themepark opened, the grounds were originally a high street of high class restaurant venues. D-Railz was transformed on top of this and finally opened in 1985. A massive expansion was completed in 2004, introducing new rollercoasters for the adult audience of D-Railz.
Today, we have a variety of diverse staff that help run our themepark for you. We proud ourselves on being inclusive and providing affordable value for our customers. Millions of kids in the Caridff area and beyond will agree with us.
